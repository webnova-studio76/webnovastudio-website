Threats & Defense Mechanisms

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks.

Types of Malware

  • Virus: Code that attaches to a file and spreads when the file is opened.
  • Worm: Standalone malware that replicates itself to spread to other computers in a network.
  • Ransomware: Encrypts your files and demands payment (Ransom) to unlock them.
  • Phishing: Fraudulent emails or sites designed to steal login credentials.

Defensive Layers

  • Firewall: A barrier that monitors and filters incoming/outgoing network traffic.
  • Encryption: Scrambling data so only authorized parties with a "key" can read it.
  • 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication): Requiring two forms of ID (Password + SMS code).

Cloud Computing Fundamentals

The Cloud is simply "someone else's computer" accessed via the Internet. It allows for on-demand delivery of computing power and storage.

IaaS

Infrastructure as a Service. Renting raw hardware/servers (e.g., AWS, Azure).

PaaS

Platform as a Service. Tools for developers to build apps (e.g., Google App Engine).

SaaS

Software as a Service. Finished apps used via a browser (e.g., Gmail, Netflix, Canva).

Scalability: The biggest advantage of the Cloud is the ability to increase storage or power instantly as your business grows.

Artificial Intelligence & Privacy

AI is the simulation of human intelligence by machines, particularly computer systems.

Machine Learning (ML)

A subset of AI where computers "learn" from patterns in data rather than following strict pre-written rules.

Digital Privacy in the AI Era

  • Data Mining: AI systems require massive amounts of personal data to "learn," raising privacy concerns.
  • Deepfakes: AI-generated videos or audio that look and sound like real people, used for misinformation.
  • Anonymization: The process of removing personal identifiers from data sets to protect user identity.
The Future: AI is being used in Cybersecurity to detect threats before they happen by recognizing unusual patterns in network traffic.
